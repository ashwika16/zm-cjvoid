About Triggered Insaan






Nischay Malhan, popularly known on the internet as Triggered Insaan is an Indian YouTuber from New Delhi who is famous for making commentaries, roasting and reaction videos on his YouTube channel. He over has 16 Million subscribers on his channel. He is one of the most popular youtuber in India. His also has a gaming channel called Live Insaan. On which he has over 8.97 Million subscribers.

Nischay malhan was born on 14 November 1995 in Delhi, India. Nischay is an engineering student .He completed his Engineering from IIITD,Delhi. He has an elder sister whose name is Prerna Malhan and she also has a youtube channel which is “Wanderers Hub”. Nischay also has younger brother whose name is Abhishek Malhan and he also has a youtube channel the name of the channel is “Fukra Insaan”.

Before starting his YouTube channel Triggered Insaan used to work as an internship in a private company for 6 months. And later he left the job and pursued his passion for video making. Triggered Insaan created his 1st YouTube channel with the title of "Nischay Malhan", where he upload his programming videos. Apart from the programming he also loves to play video games that’s why he made it his career.He then started his second YouTube named it as "Yes-Yes-Bhai", in which he made small roasting and reaction videos. He changed his channel name into "It's Nischay", and then he finally decides chooses his youtube channel's name as "Triggered Insaan".
